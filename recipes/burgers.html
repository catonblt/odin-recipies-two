Burgers

All American Burgers that will make you swear off Fast Food Forever!!

Ingredients

  • 2lbs Ground Beef
  • x6 Brioche Buns
  • x6 Lettuce Slices
  • x1 Tomato
  • x1 Onion

Steps

  1. Shape ground beef into six patties and cook with oil over Med/Hi heat until desired temperature is reached.
  2. Place cooked patties onto the Brioche buns and add condiments along with your tomatoes, onions, and lettuce.
  3. Don't forget an ice cold beer!
