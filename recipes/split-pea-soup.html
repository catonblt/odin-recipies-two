Split Pea Soup

35 Minute Split Pea Soup that you can't stop eating!!

Ingredients

  • Peas
  • Ham
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Chicken Stock

Steps

  1. Boil the peas and smash them.
  2. Cook the carrots and onions in the chicken stock.
  3. Fry the ham on a skillet.
  4. Combine smashed peas and ham with all of the chicken stock mixture and simmer on low for 20mins.
