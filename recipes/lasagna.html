Lasagna

20 Minute Lasagna that you can't stop eating!!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Spinach
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles.
  2. Layer Noodles with Ricotta, Mozzarella, and Marinara Sauce.
  3. Add a final layer of Mozzarella cheese at the top.
  4. Cook in the oven covered at 450F for 23 minutes. Uncover for the last 3 minutes of cook time.
